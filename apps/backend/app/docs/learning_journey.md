## Overview

Adnan Ahmed Bhameshan is a Cloud-Focused Full Stack Engineer based in Hyderabad, India, with interests in cloud infrastructure, DevOps, automation, AI-assisted development, and scalable software systems.

His technical journey has been defined by continuous self-learning, project-based experimentation, and a commitment to converting theoretical knowledge into working solutions.

## Educational Background

Adnan began his technical education with a Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE), where he developed analytical thinking, structured problem-solving skills, and an understanding of systems and engineering fundamentals.

He later transitioned into Information Technology through a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree at Vidya Jyothi Institute of Technology (VJIT), Hyderabad, where he expanded into software engineering, databases, networking, cloud computing, and application development.

## Learning Philosophy

Adnan believes the most effective way to learn technology is through a balance of theory and practical implementation.

His personal learning framework is:

* 30% Theory
* 70% Hands-On Practice

A principle that has stayed with him throughout his academic journey comes from his school principal:

> "Make both the paper and your hands dark with ink until the results speak for themselves."

For Adnan, this means building, testing, debugging, documenting, and improving real systems rather than relying solely on theoretical understanding.
